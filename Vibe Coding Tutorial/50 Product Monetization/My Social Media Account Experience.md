## Some Social Media Account Starting Suggestions

1) Real most important

Looking at self and entire internet observation, I discovered **more real, more natural, easier to explode**.

Doing good content, is everything's foundation. Real things in AI era will be more precious. Don't deliberately imitate others, your experience is most unique material.



2) Lower expectations, persist action

I know many friends will feel anxious because of traffic, feel things made have no one watching, no one recognizing.

But I want to say: **persist will definitely succeed.**

I just started doing public account, readership also single digits. But later I continuously 100+ days after work livestream until early morning, just this persistence, let me break through bottleneck.

Facing anxiety's best method is: lower expectations and persist action, I really like Speak Less Do More this sentence.



3) Interesting very important

For advanced programming technology or complete tutorials, people who truly want to see this content will proactively search, don't need you to turn text draft into video. Especially AI era came, some friends pursuing efficiency even won't watch long videos, but directly use AI tools summarized.

Suggest make content relaxed and interesting, let everyone in fragmented time understand new knowledge. Like I try to let every video can bring everyone some knowledge, even if "my server got attacked" this kind of hard to hold experience, believe also can let everyone step on fewer pits.



4) AI is only tool, core is your creativity

Now through AI + various workflows can quickly batch create content, although has opportunity toËµ∑Èáè, but definitely has corresponding bottleneck, because simpler things, replication cost lower.

Key still must have own creativity and characteristics, just that nowadays achieving creativity cost became simple.



5) Immediately action

Many people ask me: "I also want to do social media, but don't know how to start."

My answer is: **don't just think, go try more!**

I myself very hate that theory faction, set after set, watched then forget. Instead is self practicing account process, will naturally slowly learn much knowledge, and impression more profound. Give example, get limited flow once then you know next time copywriting need to pay attention to which forbidden words.

Also how to write title, how to make cover, how to select topics, how to see data, how to optimize content, these all learned in practice.

Practicing to waste worst case just restart!

Like my first account "Interview Monarch" was practiced to waste, but that experience let me learn too many things, also is key to later new accountËµ∑Èáè.



6) Find accurate positioning, do what you're good at

Don't compete with some field's more professional teams. Like I at start wouldn't produce dozens of hours complete courses, because training institutions more professional.

Like my early period positioning very clear: share real experience + interesting technical knowledge + quality resource recommendations.

Find your own positioning, do what you're good at, this very important.
