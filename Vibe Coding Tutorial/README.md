## What is Vibe Coding?

Simply put, **Vibe Coding is chatting with AI in plain language and having AI help you write code.** You don't need to memorize any syntax - just clearly describe your requirements, like "help me make an accounting page," and AI will generate it for you. Programming becomes as natural as chatting - this is the charm of Vibe Coding.



## Why learn Vibe Coding?

Before, learning programming took months. Now with Vibe Coding, you can get started in just a few days. Think of an idea today, make it a reality today - productivity increased dozens of times!

After learning Vibe Coding, you can quickly build small tools that improve office efficiency, develop applications that solve life's problems, and turn ideas in your mind into real products that generate profit.
